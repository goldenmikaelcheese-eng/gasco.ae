How It Works
From meter to manager in under 2 minutes
Step 1
🏢
Select Building & Unit
Worker taps unit — sees previous reading and date instantly. Green = done, yellow = flagged, white = pending.
›
Step 2
🔢
Enter Meter Reading
Large numpad for fast entry. Select Cash or Card. Tenant signs on screen with finger.
›
Step 3
🧾
Bill Calculates & Prints
Automatic bill — gas + service charge + VAT. Receipt prints in seconds. Tenant keeps a copy.

Step 4
📊
Manager Sees It Live
Dashboard updates within 10 seconds. Cash totals, building progress, flagged units — all live.
