The Problem

UAE gas billing is still
done by hand.

📓
Physical booklets per building
Every building has its own paper ledger. Workers carry it unit to unit, writing room numbers, dates, and meter values by hand.
🧮
4 minutes per unit — manual calculation
Workers subtract last month's reading, multiply by the rate, and write the total manually — for every unit, every month.
⚠️
No error checking
Arithmetic mistakes go unnoticed until billing disputes arise. No alerts, no validation, no audit trail.
📊
No data, no visibility
Paper records can't be searched, compared, or aggregated. Managers have zero insight into usage across buildings.
